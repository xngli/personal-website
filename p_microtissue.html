Microtissue culture platform

3D cell culture platform

Description

Current cell biology studies and drug testing rely on the widely-used cell culture model that grows monolayer of cells on flat plastic surfaces, such as petri dishes. While this technique is versatile and convenient, the morphology and function of cells grown on 2D surface deviate from cells in the human body, where complex 3D microenvironment and cell-cell interactions come into play. The goal of this research is to develop a 3D cell culture platform that enables the formation and stimulation of millimeter-scale 3D tissues outside the body. The platform should be easy-to-use and fully compatible with standard analytical techniques.

The prototypes of the tissue culture platform was first built using 3D printing and replica molding. A customized CNC milling schemes with accuracy of 50 µm was developed for high-precision fabrication of the mold.The tissue culture platform was used to generate tissues with stem cell derived cardiomyocytes, proving the their high relevancy in drug screening and personalized medicine.
